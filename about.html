about Amy

I'm Amy Wowak, a central administrator at Services for the Underserved. I work in all boroughs of New York City, driving scalable solutions to transform the lives of people with disabilities, people in poverty and people facing homelessness; solutions that contribute to righting societal imbalances. I work primarily in our developmental disabilites and veterans divisions.

Originally from a farm on the North Fork of Long Island, I moved to Brooklyn after completing my studies in lighting design.

When not working or learning how to code, I enjoy watching and quoting old films, reading, muay Thai, baking, and avoiding the sun.
